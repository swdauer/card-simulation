Card Simulation

An Excursion in Data Generation for Card Games

The Questions

When thinking about poker it seems to me that there is a collection of possible strategies. Some of these strategies are more profitable than others. Perhaps some are highly profitable when your opponents' are deploying a specific strategy and unprofitable other times. What I want to know is (1) how to create a profitable strategy, and (2) how to change a strategy to increase profitability depending on the actions of my opponents.
